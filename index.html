GEOG 178/258: Conceptual Modeling and Programing for the Geo-Sciences

1. Course Info

Class Name: Conceptual Modeling and Programing for the Geo-Sciences
Course: GEOG 178/258
Term: Winter 2017

Description: A project-based course introducing major conceptual modeling paradigms and object-oriented programming from a Geoinformatics perspective. The class is intended for undergraduate students from Geography and the broader Geo-Sciences who have limited (or no previous)experience in software engineering.
